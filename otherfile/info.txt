: Features of the Shooter in this program             
- spring constant: 719.43 N/m                
- shooting range:  1 to 4 m
- angle range: 15 to 80 dagree
: Note
1. The basket is 41 cm tall and the ball has a mass of 24 g.
2. This simulation is regardless of air resistance.
3. Initial position when shooting (x, y) = (0, height of shooter's end point)
4. Distance which can be set is (1) the distance of initial point and (2) the center of the basket.
